The Neighbourhood (abbreviated as the nbhd) is an American alternative rock band formed in Newbury Park, California, in 2011. The band is composed of vocalist Jesse Rutherford, guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, and bassist Mikey Margott. Drummer Brandon Fried was also part of the band from 2014 until 2022. After releasing two EPs, I'm Sorry... and Thank You, the Neighbourhood signed with Columbia Records to release their debut studio album, I Love You. (2013). "Sweater Weather", the album's third single, became their sole entry Billboard Hot 100—peaking at number 14—and re-surged in popularity nearly a decade later on radio airplay and Spotify. Other notable hits from the band include "Daddy Issues", "Softcore", "You Get Me So High", "Reflections" and "The Beach".
